Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 31, 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect the following information:

  • Account information you provide at registration (e.g. name, email address, password).
  • Expense records you create, including amounts, categories, dates, and optional notes.
  • Basic usage data such as login timestamps and page interactions, used to maintain and improve the service.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide and improve Spendly. Specifically, we use it to:

  • Authenticate you and secure your account.
  • Display your expense history and summaries within the app.
  • Diagnose bugs and improve the reliability of the service.

We do not use your data for advertising, and we do not sell or share it with third parties for commercial purposes.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on secured servers. We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect it against unauthorised access, loss, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us through the app.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly may use third-party services for infrastructure and analytics (such as hosting providers). These services are bound by their own privacy policies. We do not integrate with advertising networks or social media tracking tools.

5. Cookies

Spendly uses only essential session cookies required to keep you logged in. We do not use tracking or analytics cookies.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out through the app's support channel. We will respond as promptly as possible.
